Elysian Coffee Branding

Completed: April 2025 Client: Elysian Coffee Co. Category: Brand Identity

Project Overview

Elysian Coffee Co. approached me to create a brand identity that would position them as a premium coffee roaster in a competitive market. The client needed a complete branding system that would appeal to discerning coffee enthusiasts while maintaining an approachable feel.

The scope included logo design, packaging, stationery, digital assets, and in-store materials. The brand needed to convey quality, craftsmanship, and the company's commitment to ethical sourcing.

The Challenge

The main challenge was creating a visual identity that would stand out in the saturated specialty coffee market while avoiding clich√©s common in the industry. The design needed to balance premium positioning with accessibility to appeal to both coffee connoisseurs and casual drinkers.

Additionally, the system needed to be flexible enough to accommodate various product lines and future expansion while maintaining brand consistency.

The Solution

I developed a sophisticated visual identity centered around a custom wordmark that combines modern simplicity with subtle references to coffee's organic origins. The color palette features deep, rich tones that evoke the coffee experience, balanced with clean white space for a contemporary feel.

The packaging system uses a modular approach that maintains brand consistency while allowing each coffee variety to have its own distinct identity. Typography plays a key role in the system, with a carefully selected pairing of serif and sans-serif fonts that convey both heritage and modernity.

The digital assets translate this premium experience to screens, with a responsive website design that showcases product photography and tells the brand's story in an engaging way.
